Details

Training

Suitable for all badminton enthusiasts to learn, brushing up on skills and techniques under the guidance of our KWL coaches. Players are taught the fundamentals of the sport in a fun atmosphere that brings out their enjoyment for badminton.

Private Training

A class tailored to your individual or private group needs with personalized attention. Perfect for individuals with irregular schedules, or those who
require a more customized training regime.
